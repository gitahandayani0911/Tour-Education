Reading: Exploring Cultural Heritage in Yogyakarta

Yogyakarta is one of the most popular cultural tourism destinations in Indonesia. The city is rich in cultural sites, such as Prambanan Temple, Keraton Yogyakarta, and Taman Sari. These sites attract tourists from all over the world who want to learn about Javanese culture, architecture, and traditions.

Prambanan Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its tall and pointed Hindu-style architecture. The temple complex tells the story of Ramayana and is often used for traditional dance performances during the evening.

Keraton Yogyakarta is the royal palace of the Sultan of Yogyakarta. Visitors can see traditional Javanese performances, visit museums, and interact with local guides who share the rich history of the kingdom.

Writing: How to Write a Travel Blog

Writing a travel blog helps you share your experiences and inspire others to explore new places. Follow these steps:

  1. Choose a cultural destination you love.
  2. Describe what makes the place special.
  3. Include interesting facts or historical background.
  4. Share personal experiences and tips.
  5. Use engaging language and visuals.

Example: “Last month, I visited Prambanan Temple. The stunning sunset behind the temple towers took my breath away. Don’t forget to watch the Ramayana Ballet—it’s magical!”

Assignment (HOTs)

Task: Imagine you're a tour guide in your hometown. Create a short blog post (150–200 words) to promote a cultural site. Explain what makes the site special, what tourists can do there, and why it's worth visiting.

Skills used: Analyzing cultural value, synthesizing ideas, evaluating tourism potential, writing creatively.
